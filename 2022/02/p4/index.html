NFTs

On Saturday

Everyday, there’s a new crypto word and suddenly everyone seems to understand what it means. It’s on all social media platforms, the big wigs start talking about it and you begin to feel left out. Recently, that new word has been NFTs. In this article, I intend to break down what NFTs are, and show you how you can get involved in the NFT movement.

NFTs stands for Non-Fungible Tokens. A fungible item is an item that can be used to replace another item of the same value. In even simpler words, a $1 bill is fungible with another $1 bill. Thus, NFTs are cryptographic tokens with unique data and metadata that are stored on a digital ledger, and cannot be interchanged with another cryptographic token. While 1 Bitcoin is equal to another Bitcoin, no two NFTs are the same.

I heard NFTs are like selling artwork?

Yes, the recent hype around NFTs has been about artworks. A digital artist by the name Beeple sold a group of NFTs for over $69 million. These artworks were a compilation of his first 5,000 days of work and have been recorded as one of the most expensive set of NFT artworks sold till date.
